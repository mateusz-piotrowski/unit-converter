# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our project seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, we encourage you to report it to us as soon as possible.  
We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings.

### How to Report

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to `security@example.com` with the following information:

-   A detailed description of the vulnerability, including its nature and potential impact.
-   Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
-   Any proof-of-concept code or exploits (if applicable).
-   Your contact information, so we can reach out to you for further details.

### Our Commitment

-   We will acknowledge receipt of your report within [e.g., 2 business days].
-   We will investigate the reported vulnerability promptly.
-   We will keep you informed of our progress in addressing the vulnerability.
-   We will credit you for your responsible disclosure, if you wish.

## Responsible Disclosure

We request that you do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had a reasonable amount of time to address it.  
Public disclosure before a fix is available could put users at risk.

Thank you for helping us keep our project secure!
